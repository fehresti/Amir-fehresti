# Amir Fehresti

Amir Fehresti is a fintech entrepreneur, data scientist, and software engineer with a strong focus on building large-scale, non-bank financial platforms and advanced technology systems.

He is the **Founder and CEO of LOIS Group**, a technology-driven ecosystem developing fintech orchestration platforms, payment solutions, and blockchain-based financial infrastructures without operating as a bank or regulated financial institution.

He is also the **Director General of the International Center for Nanosystems and Quantum Technologies**, based in **Tbilisi, Georgia**, where he leads strategic research initiatives and international scientific collaborations in nanotechnology and quantum technologies.
